# Contributing

LongFeedback is research software. A change is ready for review when it preserves
the scientific contracts, includes tests for new invariants, and passes all local
quality checks listed in the README.

Keep raw interaction data, credentials, and generated artifacts out of Git. New
datasets require a versioned source manifest and privacy review. Use “predictive
contribution” for observational data unless the identification assumptions for
causal credit are explicit and defensible.
